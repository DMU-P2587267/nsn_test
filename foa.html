Future of anime

As anime is increasing worldwide and it is shown as being used as brand ambassadors by being printed on clothes, created into games, made into smaller spin off shows, the presence of anime increases everyday.

This means that anime is most likely to keep growing its fanbase in the future as the global anime market was at $22.6 billion in 2020 and is said to be going up to at least $48.3 billion by 2030 which is only 7 years away from now.
